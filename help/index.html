MassCPR Federated Data Network Help

About the Network
The MassCPR Federated Data Network combines the aggregate results of analyses that are performed locally within each participating institution using electronic health record (EHR) data. By leveraging the collective informatics capabilities of the consortium, this network enables data discovery and sharing while maintaining institutional autonomy and patient privacy.

Terms of Query Access

QUALIFIED USERS: Access to the MassCPR Federated Data Network is limited to faculty with a primary appointment at or above the Instructor level at Beth Israel Lahey Health (BILH), Dana-Farber Cancer Institute (DFCI) or Mass General Brigham (MGB).

AUTHORIZED USE CASES: Use of the MassCPR Federated Data Network is restricted to research and public health use cases related to infectious diseases. All queries are archived for monitoring and auditing purposes and are linked to your account.

OBFUSCATION: Queries will return only aggregate counts and summary statistics. Queries resulting in small numbers of patients will return blurred or obfuscated estimates to prevent inadvertent disclosure of individually identifiable patient information. Any attempt to circumvent these safeguards will be reported to the Network Oversight Committee and may result in your account being deactivated.

IDENTIFICATION: You may not, either alone or in combination with other information, attempt to identify or contact any individual who is or may be a source of the data. If you inadvertently receive identifiable information or otherwise identify an individual, you must promptly notify us at contact@catalyst.harvard.edu.

CITATIONS: Network Sites may not be individually named in any publication using network data without permission. If permission isn’t granted, sites must be referenced as “Site 1, Site 2, …”. Any intellectual property, including but not limited to patents, that is derived from the use of the Network, as well as publications that include Network Data, must acknowledge the MassCPR Federated Data Network.

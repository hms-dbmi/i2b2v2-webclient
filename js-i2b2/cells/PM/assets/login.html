The MassCPR Federated Data Network combines the aggregate results of analyses that are performed locally within each participating institution using electronic health record (EHR) data. By leveraging the collective informatics capabilities of the consortium, this network enables data discovery and sharing while maintaining institutional autonomy and patient privacy. Learn more
